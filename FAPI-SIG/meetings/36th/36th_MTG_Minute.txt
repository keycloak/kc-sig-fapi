Summary :

 * OID-F Certification: Australia Consumer Data Right (CDR)
   Keycloak 15.0.2 got certified as FAPI OpenID Provider for Australia CDR.
   https://openid.net/certification/#FAPI_OPs

 * OID-F Certification: OpenID Connect
   Keycloak 18.0.0 got re-certified as OpenID Provider.
   https://openid.net/certification/#OPs

 * OID-F Certification: OpenID Connect for Logout Profiles
   Keycloak 18.0.0 got certified as OpenID Provider for Logout Profiles.
   https://openid.net/certification/#OPs

 * OID-F Certification: Summary of Certifications Keycloak has achieved
   Keycloak has achieved 6 of 7 certifications:
   - OpenID Provider
   - OpenID Provider for Logout Profile
   - FAPI OpenID Provider for FAPI 1 Advanced Final
   - FAPI OpenID Provider for Australia CDR
   - FAPI OpenID Provider for Brazil Open Banking
   - FAPI-CIBA OpenID Provider
   Keycloak has not yet achieved 1 remaining certification:
   - FAPI OpenID Provider for UK Open Banking

 * Presentations about keycloak, FAPI, and FAPI-SIG
   Marek has FAPI presentation on DevConf mini in Brno, Czech Republic on 2 Jun.
   "Secure digital transformation via keycloak's FAPI"
   https://devconfczmini2022.sched.com/event/11l61/secure-digital-transformation-via-keycloaks-fapi
   Takashi has also keycloak and FAPI presentation on Open Identity Summit 2022 in Lyngby, Denmark on 7 Jul.
   "Flexible Method for Supporting OAuth 2.0 Based Security Profiles in Keycloak"
   https://oid2022.compute.dtu.dk/prog.html
   It might be good to gather these presentation materials and are stored in FAPI-SIG's repository as a knowledge base.
